Présentation

Capgemini Architecture Week 2015 is a Business & Technology Event presented worldwide, concentrated in one week, across different cities with local events and webcasts to connect Customers, Partners, Capgemini architects and IT Leaders around Technology Innovation & Business Value.

 

This year’s motto is "Fluid & Predictive Enterprises" and during the week you will hear insights on:

  • Digital Innovation
  • Third Platform (Social, Mobile, Analytics, Cloud)
  • FOG Cloud, Internet of Everything & Predictive Enterprises 
  • DevOps, API Factories & Continuous IT
  • Business Fluidity

 
It is a unique opportunity to :

  • Discover Capgemini architecture & consulting projects and benefit from our experience, customer feedback, debates and technology leaders;
  • Meet enterprise architects, business consultants and technology leaders
  • See our Innovation’ Labs worldwide

During the week there are many different ways to discover, share & learn:

  • Streams dedicated to :
    • Customer business stakes and Capgemini answers 
    • Methodologies and expertise
    • Mutualization of Capgemini and partner skills
  • Special areas dedicated to :
    • Debates
    • Expert's Club and One to One meetings
